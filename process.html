The Riments Method

Every project at Riments follows a disciplined experimentation cycle. The goal is not simply to build systems, but to understand their behavior, analyze outcomes, and refine engineering decisions through iteration.

01

Define the Question

Every experiment begins with a precise engineering question. Understanding what needs to be tested gives direction to the system design.

02

Design the Experiment

Architecture and constraints are defined to create a controlled environment where system behavior can be measured.

03

Build the Prototype

Hardware and software components are integrated into a working prototype capable of interacting with real signals.

04

Observe & Analyze

Performance and unexpected behaviors are studied carefully. Each observation reveals deeper insights into the system.

05

Refine the System

Based on insights, the system is improved and optimized. This cycle continues until the system achieves clarity and reliability.
